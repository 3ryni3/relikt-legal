§ 02

Description of service

RELIKT is a field tool for historical exploration enthusiasts and metal detector users. The App enables:

  • Recording and documenting field sessions with GPS tracking
  • Cataloguing finds (category, depth, photos, notes)
  • Viewing historical maps and orthophoto layers
  • Generating reports for heritage authority documentation
  • Verifying cadastral parcel boundaries (ULDK API)
  • Managing session history and exporting data
