§ 07

Security

  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt — never stored in plain text

§ 08

Cookies and localStorage

RELIKT uses browser localStorage to store:

  • Session token (authentication)
  • User preferences (recording mode, map settings)
  • Find data in offline mode (local cache)

The app does not use tracking or marketing cookies.
